Welcome to Elcom!

Welcome to our website, where we delve into the fascinating world of the elements and compounds essential to the human body. Discover the vital roles played by elements such as Zinc, Phosphorus, Potassium, Iron, and Calcium, as well as compounds like Carbon Dioxide, Cholesterol, Citric Acid, Water, and Sodium Chloride.

Explore how these elements and compounds function within our bodies, their various effects, and how we can obtain them through our diet and lifestyle. Learn about their importance, their sources, and their impact on our health.

Dive into our website created for understanding the chemistry of life and how these fundamental substances keep us healthy and functioning!

What is the purpose of this website?

This website is for educational purposes only. It is where you can see, learn, and know more about the elements and compounds that you will find in your body. It is also designed to expose students or people of any age to be more aware of their body.

You can expect to leave our website with lots of new knowledge, such as learning how important these elements and compounds truly are and how they can make changes in our body. You can expect to learn how to take care of your body more and how we can avoid or stop doing things that are not good for our health. Moreover, we can also stop ourselves from eating unhealthy foods and avoid adding seasonings/condiments to our food that are not suitable for our body when cooking.
